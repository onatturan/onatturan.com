Bio – About Me

My name is Onat Turan, a passionate software developer and athlete who thrives on challenges. My journey started in sports, where I played basketball and American football at the college level before fully committing to bodybuilding. I have spent over a decade training like a soldier—physically and mentally—always pushing my limits. However, my passion extends beyond fitness; I am deeply fascinated by technology and software development.

I pursued Computer Programming and Analysis at George Brown College, where I developed expertise in full-stack development, artificial intelligence, and data science. I love solving complex problems, optimizing systems, and building innovative projects that merge human performance with cutting-edge technology. My long-term goal is to create humanoid robots that assist people in their daily lives, making technology more intuitive and accessible.

Every line of code I write and every rep I complete in the gym reflects my relentless pursuit of growth. Whether I’m building a new application, analyzing data, or training for a competition, I believe in constantly evolving and pushing the boundaries of what’s possible.
